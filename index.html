Our Mission Statement


Team 4541’s mission is to instruct and inspire the next generation of engineers. We use the FIRST Robotics Competition program to teach our students about the applications of engineering and business in the most practical manner available. With the establishment of the Catholic Robotics League in 2017, Team 4541 aims to share the lessons that FIRST has taught us with as many students as possible. We have ignited a spark of inspiration in our community, and now it is our team’s responsibility to fan the flame.
